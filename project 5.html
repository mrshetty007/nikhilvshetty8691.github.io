HR ANALYTICS DASHBOARD

Project Overview

The HR Analytics Dashboard provides a comprehensive view of employee attrition and satisfaction within an organization. This dashboard visualizes key metrics such as total employees, attrition count, attrition rate, active employees, and average age. The dashboard includes detailed insights into department-wise attrition, employee distribution by age and gender, job satisfaction ratings across various roles, and education field-specific attrition rates.

Key Responsibilities

. Monitor Employee Attrition: Track overall and department-wise attrition to identify patterns and areas of concern.

. Analyze Demographic Data: Assess the distribution of employees by age and gender to understand workforce composition.

. Reduce manual effort in preparing "Ticket creation data."

. Evaluate Job Satisfaction: Measure job satisfaction across different roles to pinpoint areas for improvement.

.Identify Trends: Utilize visualizations to detect trends in attrition by gender, age group, and education field.

Improve HR Processes: Use insights from the dashboard to enhance HR strategies and reduce attrition rates.

Files and Tools Involved

. Power BI

. Excel

. AI Tool(Chat GPT)

. Share point to store all the data

. Main Data base is service now

Process

.Data Collection: Gather employee and attrition data from ServiceNow and other relevant sources.

.Data Preparation: Preprocess the data in Excel to ensure accuracy and completeness.

.Data Upload: Upload the prepared data to SharePoint for centralized access.

.Dashboard Creation: Connect the data to Power BI and create visualizations based on the specified metrics.

.Analysis and Insights: Analyze the visualizations to derive actionable insights and identify key trends in employee attrition and satisfaction.

Total Employees: Display the current total number of employees.

Attrition Count: Show the number of employees who have left the organization.

Attrition Rate: Calculate the percentage of employees who have left the organization.

Active Employees: Display the current number of active employees.

Average Age: Show the average age of employees.

Dept Wise Attrition: Visualize the attrition rates across different departments.

Employee Distribution by Age and Gender: Show the number of employees by age group and gender.

Job Satisfaction Rating: Display job satisfaction ratings across various roles.

Sum of Attrition: Visualize the total attrition count across different education fields.

Attrition Rate by Gender and Age Group: Show attrition rates segmented by gender and age group.

Result

The HR Analytics Dashboard provided critical insights into employee attrition and satisfaction, enabling the organization to address key issues and improve HR processes. The automation and dynamic visualizations offered by Power BI reduced manual effort and errors, leading to more accurate and reliable data. These insights contributed to better decision-making and helped in implementing strategies to enhance employee retention and satisfaction.
